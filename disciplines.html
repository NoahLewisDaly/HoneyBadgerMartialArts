Our Disciplines

Jiu-Jitsu

Jiu-Jitsu is a grappling sport focused on control, position, and submissions. The goal is to take the fight to the ground, gain dominant positions, and finish with joint locks or chokes.

Muay Thai

Thailand's national martial art, known as the "Art of Eight Limbs" for its powerful use of fists, elbows, knees, and shins, combined with clinching and sweeps.

Judo

Judo, meaning "gentle way," is a Japanese martial art and combat sport focusing on throws, grappling, and pins using an opponent's force against them, emphasizing leverage, efficiency, and discipline over brute strength.

Taekwondo

Korean martial art and Olympic sport emphasizing dynamic kicking, spinning kicks, and fast hand techniques, focusing on physical and mental discipline.
